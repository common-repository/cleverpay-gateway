Hello Clever Gateway for WooCommerce is a plugin that helps extend WooCommerce, allowing users to take payments via Hello Clever portal.

== Description ==

### **Hello Clever provides a safer, faster and smarter way for Australian store owners to get paid.**

Hello Clever’s Buy-to-Earn Payment Gateway is the first of its kind for all Shopify/WooCommerce merchants to offer cashback at the point of checkout. 

### Merchant Benefits

1. Acquire New Customers
2. Free Marketing Assets
3. One-Click Purchase with PayTo
4. Reduce Abandoned Carts
5. Incentivise with Rewards
6. Real-Time Settlements with PayID & PayTo
7. Free Listing on the Hello Clever App Marketplace
8. Create Campaigns on Demand

### Payment Options Accepted

1. PayID
2. PayTo
3. Apple Pay
4. Google Pay
5. Credit & Debit Cards

== Installation ==

1. Login to your WordPress admin.
2. Navigate to “Plugins > Add New”.
3. Type “Hello Clever” into the Keyword search box and press the Enter key.
4. Find the “Hello Clever Gateway for WooCommerce ” plugin. Note: the plugin is made by “Hello Clever”.
5. Click the “Install Now” button.
6. Click the “Activate” button.
7. Navigate to “WooCommerce > Settings”.
8. Click the “Payments” tab.
9. Click the “Hello Clever Gateway – Hello Clever” sub-tab.
10. Enter the App ID and Secret Key that was provided by Hello Clever for Production use.
11. Save changes
